To create a proper favicon.ico:

  1. Take a screenshot of the blue square above
  2. Use an online favicon generator (like favicon.io or realfavicongenerator.net)
  3. Upload the image and generate favicon.ico
  4. Place the generated favicon.ico in your public folder
  5. Update layout.tsx to use '/favicon.ico' instead of '/favicon.svg'
